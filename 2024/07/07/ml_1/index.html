线性回归

一元线性回归

线性回归试图找到一条直线，使得f(xi)=wxi+bf(x_i)=wx_i+bf(xi​)=wxi​+b，www、bbb是模型的参数。假设yiy_iyi​是真实值，f(xi)f(x_i)f(xi​)是预测值，那么E(w,b)E(w, b)E(w,b)表示模型预测值和真实值的误差。

E(w,b)=∑i=1m(yi−f(xi))2=∑i=1m(yi−wxi−b)2\begin{aligned} E(w, b) &= \sum_{i=1}^{m} (y_i - f(x_i))^2 \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{m} (y_i - wx_i - b)^2 \end{aligned} E(w,b)​=i=1∑m​(yi​−f(xi​))2=i=1∑m​(yi​−wxi​−b)2​

基于均方误差最小化来进行求解模型的方法称为“最小二乘法”。

∂E(w,b)∂w=∂∂w[∑i=1m(yi−wxi−b)2]=∑i=1m∂∂w(yi−wxi−b)2=∑i=1m2(yi−wxi−b)(−xi)=2(w∑i=1mxi2−∑i=1m(yi−b)xi)∂E(w,b)∂b=∂∂b[∑i=1m(yi−wxi−b)2]=∑i=1m∂∂b(yi−wxi−b)2=∑i=1m2(yi−wxi−b)(−1)=∑i=1m2(b−yi+wxi)=2(∑i=1mb−∑i=1myi+∑i=1mwxi)=2(mb−∑i=1m(yi−wxi))\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial E_{(w, b)}}{\partial w} &= \frac{\partial}{\partial w}[\sum_{i=1}^{m}(y_i-wx_i-b)^2] \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{m} \frac{\partial}{\partial w}(y_i-wx_i-b)^2 \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{m} 2(y_i-wx_i-b)(-x_i) \\ &= 2(w\sum_{i=1}^{m}x_i^2-\sum_{i=1}^{m}(y_i-b)x_i) \\ \frac{\partial E_{(w, b)}}{\partial b} &= \frac{\partial}{\partial b}[\sum_{i=1}^{m}(y_i-wx_i-b)^2] \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{m} \frac{\partial}{\partial b}(y_i-wx_i-b)^2 \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{m} 2(y_i-wx_i-b)(-1) \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^{m} 2(b-y_i+wx_i) \\ &= 2(\sum_{i=1}^{m}b-\sum_{i=1}^{m}y_i+\sum_{i=1}^{m}wx_i) \\ &= 2(mb - \sum_{i=1}^{m}(y_i-wx_i)) \\ \end{aligned} ∂w∂E(w,b)​​∂b∂E(w,b)​​​=∂w∂​[i=1∑m​(yi​−wxi​−b)2]=i=1∑m​∂w∂​(yi​−wxi​−b)2=i=1∑m​2(yi​−wxi​−b)(−xi​)=2(wi=1∑m​xi2​−i=1∑m​(yi​−b)xi​)=∂b∂​[i=1∑m​(yi​−wxi​−b)2]=i=1∑m​∂b∂​(yi​−wxi​−b)2=i=1∑m​2(yi​−wxi​−b)(−1)=i=1∑m​2(b−yi​+wxi​)=2(i=1∑m​b−i=1∑m​yi​+i=1∑m​wxi​)=2(mb−i=1∑m​(yi​−wxi​))​

根据∂E(w,b)∂w=0\frac{\partial E_{(w, b)}}{\partial w} =0∂w∂E(w,b)​​=0 和 ∂E(w,b)∂b=0\frac{\partial E_{(w, b)}}{\partial b}=0∂b∂E(w,b)​​=0 即可求出参数www 和 bbb。

多元线性回归

如果有多个特征，那么线性回归的模型可以写成：

f(xi)=wTxi+b f(x_i) = \bm{w}^T\bm{x_i} + b f(xi​)=wTxi​+b

类似的，利用最小二乘法对参数w\bm{w}w进行估计。

(w∗,b∗)=arg⁡min⁡(w,b)∑i=1m(f(xi)−yi)2=arg⁡min⁡(w,b)∑i=1m(yi−f(xi))2=arg⁡min⁡(w,b)∑i=1m(yi−(wTxi+b))2\begin{aligned} (\bm{w}^*, b^*) &= \arg\min_{(\bm{w}, b)} \sum_{i=1}^m (f(x_i) - y_i)^2 \\ &= \arg\min_{(\bm{w}, b)} \sum_{i=1}^m (y_i - f(x_i))^2 \\ &= \arg\min_{(\bm{w}, b)} \sum_{i=1}^m (y_i - (\bm{w}^T\bm{x_i} + b))^2 \end{aligned} (w∗,b∗)​=arg(w,b)min​i=1∑m​(f(xi​)−yi​)2=arg(w,b)min​i=1∑m​(yi​−f(xi​))2=arg(w,b)min​i=1∑m​(yi​−(wTxi​+b))2​

为了计算方便，我们做以下替换：

w^=(w1w2⋮wdb)xi^=(xi1xi2⋮xid1)f(xi)=(w1w2⋯wdwd+1)(xi1xi2⋮xid1)f(xi^)=w^Txi^\begin{aligned} \hat{\bm{w}} &= \begin{pmatrix} w_1 \\ w_2 \\ \vdots \\ w_d \\ b \end{pmatrix} \\ \\ \hat{\bm{x_i}} &= \begin{pmatrix} \bm{x_{i1}} \\ \bm{x_{i2}} \\ \vdots \\ \bm{x_{id}} \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} \\ f(x_i) &= \begin{pmatrix} w_1 & w_2 & \cdots & w_d & w_{d+1}\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x_{i1} \\ x_{i2} \\ \vdots \\ x_{id} \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} \\ f(\hat{\bm{x_i}}) &= \hat{\bm{w}}^T \hat{\bm{x_i}} \end{aligned} w^xi​^​f(xi​)f(xi​^​)​=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎛​w1​w2​⋮wd​b​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎞​=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎛​xi1​xi2​⋮xid​1​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎞​=(w1​​w2​​⋯​wd​​wd+1​​)⎝⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎜⎛​xi1​xi2​⋮xid​1​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎟⎞​=w^Txi​^​​

因此，我们可以得到：

w^∗=arg⁡min⁡w^∑i=1m(yi−w^Txi^)2=arg⁡min⁡w^∑i=1m(yi−xi^Tw^)2=arg⁡min⁡w^(y1−x1^Tw^,⋯ ,ym−xm^Tw^)(y1−x1^Tw^⋮ym−xm^Tw^)=arg⁡min⁡w^(y−Xw^)T(y−Xw^)\begin{aligned} \hat{\bm{w}}^* &= \arg\min_{\hat{\bm{w}}} \sum_{i=1}^m (y_i - \hat{\bm{w}}^T\hat{\bm{x_i}})^2 \\ &= \arg\min_{\hat{\bm{w}}} \sum_{i=1}^m (y_i - \hat{\bm{x_i}}^T\hat{\bm{w}})^2 \\ &= \arg\min_{\hat{\bm{w}}} \begin{pmatrix} y_1 - \hat{\bm{x_1}}^T\hat{\bm{w}}, & \cdots, & y_m - \hat{\bm{x_m}}^T\hat{\bm{w}} \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} y_1 - \bm{\hat{x_1}^T\hat{w}} \\ \vdots \\ y_m - \bm{\hat{x_m}^T\hat{w}} \\ \end{pmatrix} \\ &= \arg\min_{\hat{\bm{w}}}(\bm{y}-\bm{X\hat{w}})^T(\bm{y}-\bm{X\hat{w}}) \\ \end{aligned} w^∗​=argw^min​i=1∑m​(yi​−w^Txi​^​)2=argw^min​i=1∑m​(yi​−xi​^​Tw^)2=argw^min​(y1​−x1​^​Tw^,​⋯,​ym​−xm​^​Tw^​)⎝⎜⎜⎛​y1​−x1​^​Tw^⋮ym​−xm​^​Tw^​⎠⎟⎟⎞​=argw^min​(y−Xw^)T(y−Xw^)​

其中，

X=(x11x12⋯x1d1⋮⋮⋱⋮⋮xm1xm2⋯xmd1)=(x1T⋮xmT)\begin{aligned} \bm{X} &= \begin{pmatrix} x_{11} & x_{12} & \cdots & x_{1d} & 1 \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\ x_{m1} & x_{m2} & \cdots & x_{md} & 1 \\ \end{pmatrix} \\ &= \begin{pmatrix} \bm{x}_1^T \\ \vdots \\ \bm{x}_m^T \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned} X​=⎝⎜⎜⎛​x11​⋮xm1​​x12​⋮xm2​​⋯⋱⋯​x1d​⋮xmd​​1⋮1​⎠⎟⎟⎞​=⎝⎜⎜⎛​x1T​⋮xmT​​⎠⎟⎟⎞​​

令

Ew^=(y−Xw^)T(y−Xw^)\begin{aligned} E_{\hat{\bm{w}}} &= (\bm{y}-\bm{X\hat{w}})^T(\bm{y}-\bm{X\hat{w}}) \end{aligned} Ew^​​=(y−Xw^)T(y−Xw^)​

对w^\hat{\bm{w}}w^求导，得

∂Ew^∂w^=2XT(Xw^−y) \frac{\partial E_{\hat{\bm{w}}}}{\partial \hat{\bm{w}}} = 2\bm{X}^T(\bm{X\hat{w}}-\bm{y}) ∂w^∂Ew^​​=2XT(Xw^−y)

当上面的式子为0时，可求得参数

w^=(XTX)−1XTy\hat{\bm{w}} = (\bm{X}^T\bm{X})^{-1} \bm{X}^T \bm{y} w^=(XTX)−1XTy
